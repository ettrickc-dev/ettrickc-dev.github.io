Instructions

  1. Review the preview carefully and make sure the names, county, and index number are correct.
  2. Print the completed answer.
  3. Sign the answer. If the complaint you received was verified, sign in front of a notary.
  4. Make copies for yourself, the plaintiff’s attorney, and any other parties.
  5. Have someone other than you mail or deliver a copy to the plaintiff’s attorney.
  6. File the answer with the court clerk. Bring or file proof of service if required.

Do not ignore deadlines. Missing the deadline can lead to a default judgment.

Definition of key terms

  • Service defenses: Reasons you challenge how you were served with court papers.
  • Defenses: Legal reasons the plaintiff should not win all or part of the claim.
  • Other reason / extra defense: Additional defense not listed in the standard checkboxes.
  • Only source of income that is exempt from collection (optional): Protected income (for example certain benefits) that may be shielded from collection.
  • Counterclaim amount (optional): Dollar amount you claim the plaintiff owes you.
  • Counterclaim reason (optional): Why you believe the plaintiff is liable to you.
  • Short explanation for your case (optional): Brief summary of your side of the case in plain language.
